Crystal Collectors!

As a Crystal Collector, you will be given a random target quota at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will collect a specific amount of points to your Current Quota.

You complete the game by matching your Current Quota to the random Target Quota. You fail the game if your Current Quota goes above the random Target Quota.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
